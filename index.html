ABOUT THE VICTORIA FALLS

Victoria Falls is one of the most beautiful natural wonders in Africa. Located on the Zambezi River, it attracts visitors from all over the world. Guests can enjoy breathtaking views, adventure activities, boat cruises, wildlife experiences and cultural tours around Livingstone and nearby areas.
